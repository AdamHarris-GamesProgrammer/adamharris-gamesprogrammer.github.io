Prosperity

Prosperity is a simple idle-based game. The objective is to slowly industralize the Earth, as the player gets further in the industry systems the earth's texture changes to show the damage that was done. This was created for Ludum Dare 45 with the theme "Start With Nothing".

Made in Unity 2019.1

Development Time: 48 hours in October 2019

The games features include:

  • Idle-based gameplay
  • Resource Management

Super Mario Bros Parody

During my first year of university I was tasked with creating a 2d game framework in SDL/C++ and use it to create a parody of the original Super Mario Bros game. I went beyond the specification we were given and added additional features such as; tile-mapping from a file, paralax background scrolling, a entity component system and a 5 levels.

Development time: 13 weeks

Grade recieved: 100/100

Starhopper OpenGL game

During my first year of university I was tasked with creating a 3d graphics simulation in OpenGL Core, this simulation had a few requirements such as loading data from a text file for meshes, and use of basic texturing. I added additional features to this such as a OBJ loader, collisions based on AABB bounding boxes, and also a simple game. The game I created in my framework was a timed-collection game in which the player had to collect as many "Star Stones" in 30 seconds as they could when the player collided with them it would add one to the score and then move it to another location. There was also a highscore system implemented to allow for players to compete with each other.

Development time: 10 weeks

Grade recieved: 81/100
